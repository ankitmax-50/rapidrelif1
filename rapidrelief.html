Built for the worst day. Designed like the best product.

Every second matters. RapidRelief unifies the entire emergency response stack — from SOS to shelter — into one beautifully simple workflow.

Instant SOS

One-tap distress signal with geolocation, severity and live updates streamed to nearby responders.
